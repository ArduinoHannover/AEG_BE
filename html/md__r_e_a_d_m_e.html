AEG BE LCD Display Library
AEG BE LCD Driver

This is an Adafruit GFX based LCD driver for AEG (BMG|MIS) LCDs, primary for BE10 and BE11. Other display types are not tested.

Wiring

The displays have two connectors at each side. On on the top left and one on the bottom right. The following pinout starts from the inner (LCD) to outer:

| Pin | Direction | Function | Ratings | | —: | — | — | — | | 1 | in | Data | | 2 | pwr | VCC | 4.5 - 5.5 V | 3 | pwr | VDD | 4.5 - 12 V | 4 | in | Reset (Enable) | | 5 | in | Switch frequency | 30 - 100 Hz | 6 | in | Load (Latch) | | 7 | in | Clock |

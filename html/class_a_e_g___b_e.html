Detailed Description

Display driver for various LCD produced by AEG (BMG|MIS)

This driver supports different types of LCDs with 40 bit shift registers (COG). Currently officially supported are BE10 (29x24px) and BE11 (39x24px). Pinout of these displays is as following (starting from inside the LCD):

1 [input] DATA

2 [power] 5V

3 [power] 10V

4 [power] GND

5 [input] ENABLE

6 [input] LCD CLOCK

7 [input] LATCH

8 [input] DATA CLOCK

9 [output] DATA
